OUR MISSION

Sparking creativity, one supply at a time

Many childcare centers and daycares struggle to provide basic school and art supplies. Without crayons, paper, and notebooks, young children miss out on critical learning experiences.

Kindness Supplies — Makayla's Care Project — bridges that gap by collecting donations from individuals and organizations and delivering them directly to the centers that need them most.

How to Help

There are many ways to support Kindness Supplies. Whether you donate a single box of crayons or organize a community drive, every contribution matters.

Donate Supplies

Until we get an established drop-off location, please contact us to drop off new or gently used school and art supplies. Every supply counts!

Organize a Drive

Corporate Partnership

Businesses and organizations can sponsor supply drives or make bulk donations. Let's create lasting impact together.

Supplies We Accept

  • Crayons (8-pack or larger)
  • Washable markers
  • Colored pencils
  • Construction paper
  • Notebooks & composition books
  • Pencils and erasers
  • Child-safe scissors
  • Glue sticks
  • Watercolor paint sets
  • Stickers and stamps
